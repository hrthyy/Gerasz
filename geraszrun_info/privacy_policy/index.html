Privacy Policy for Gerasz Run

Effective Date: 2025.01.15.

At Gerasz Run ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you play our Android game on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

When you play Gerasz Run, we collect the following information:

• Player Name: To personalize your experience and identify you in the game.

• Scores: To track and display your scores on the leaderboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

• Leaderboard: To display player names and scores on the game’s leaderboard.

• Improving Gameplay: To enhance the gaming experience and optimize the game.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except for the purpose of displaying your scores on the leaderboard within the game.
Your information is never shared with advertisers or other external parties.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
However, no data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Retention of Information

We will retain your player name and scores for displaying them on the game’s leaderboard, even after you uninstalled the game.
However, old scores that are deemed to be insignificant are deleted during maintenance, which we do regularly.

If you would like to request deletion of your data, you can contact us at geraszrundev@freemail.hu.

6. Your Choices

• Opt-Out: If you do not want to have your name and scores displayed on the leaderboard, you can choose not to participate in the leaderboard feature

• Data Deletion: If you would like to request deletion of your data, you can contact us at geraszrundev@freemail.hu.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.
We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: geraszrundev@gmail.com

By playing Gerasz Run, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
